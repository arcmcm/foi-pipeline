Dear Glasgow City Council,

I am writing to request the following information under the Freedom of Information Act.

A) The number of people who have been housed in temporary accommodation, who made homelessness or emergency applications.

B) The number of people in each of the different types of temporary accommodation who made homelessness or emergency applications.
By 'type of temporary accommodation' I mean hostels, B and Bs, council properties, private rentals, etc.

C) The average length of time people spent in temporary accommodation who made homelessness or emergency applications.

D) The average wait time to be re-housed in permanent accommodation for 2 and 3 bedroom properties. (Please provide each size of property seperately)
